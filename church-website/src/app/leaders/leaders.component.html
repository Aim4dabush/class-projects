Pastor Dan and Audra Coburn

Dan and Audra Coburn are licensed and ordained Pastors with the International Church of the Foursquare Gospel. In the past 20 years they have served as Youth Pastor, Kid's Pastor, Administrative Pastor, and now as Lead Pastors at City Church Stockton. Dan and Audra have been married over 20 years and have 4 children: Joe(23), Mikalya(18), Gabriella(14), and Levi(10). We want you to know that at City Church we are "real" people, doing life together with the HOPE of Jesus Christ! Come say "hi" and lets have lunch together!
